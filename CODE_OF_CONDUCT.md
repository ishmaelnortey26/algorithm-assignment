# Contributor Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of this project, we pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

We commit to:
- Using respectful and inclusive language
- Being welcoming and supportive
- Providing constructive feedback

We will not tolerate:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Abusive language or personal attacks
- Disrespect towards others

By participating, you agree to follow this code. Violations may be reported to the repository owner and appropriate actions will be taken.
